I am Ph.D. student in economics at [Tallinn University of Technology](http://www.ttu.ee/en/). My research interest include monetary economics, international macroeconomics, macro labour economics and the study of income, wealth and their distributions.

 I currently work as a junior economist at the [Bank of Estonia](https://www.eestipank.ee/en).
