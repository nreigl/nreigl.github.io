Welcome

I am Ph.D. student in economics at Tallinn University of Technology under the supervision of Lenno Uusküla, Ph.D. and Karsten Staehr, Ph.D. My research interest include monetary economics, international macroeconomics, macro labour economics and the study of income, wealth and their distributions.

Born in Vienna/Austria, I'm now living in Tallinn in Estonia, working as junior economist at Bank of Estonia.

I code most happily in R, Python and Julia.

Everything on this site reflects my personal views only.
